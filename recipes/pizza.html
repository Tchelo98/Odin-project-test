pizza


Description

A inovative and basic recipe for a delicious homemade pizza.

Your family will go crazy over how good this pizza is, trust me you'll want to make this every day.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • water
  • salt
  • tomato sauce
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. mix flour with water
  2. mix the salt with the water and flour
  3. throw it up in the sky till round
  4. put sauce on top
  5. put pepperoni
  6. bake
  7. eat!!
