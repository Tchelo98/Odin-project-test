Bread



Description

A very easy bread recipe that anyone can do at home with very simple ingredients and absolutly no experience.

Your family will enjoy and be shocked at how good you are in the kitchen.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • salt
  • sugar
  • happiness

Steps

  1. mix flour with water
  2. stir in a pint of salt
  3. add the sugar
  4. make into bread shape
  5. add to oven for 30 min
  6. enjoy :)
  7. be good
